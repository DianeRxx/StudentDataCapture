Technology Graduate Scheme

Our full-time Technology Analyst Development Programme will place you at the epicentre of an organisation where fast-changing business priorities and market conditions call for constant innovation and cutting-edge technologies. We recruit a significant number of people from technical and analytical backgrounds with an interest and aptitude for technology. We also welcome graduates from mathematical and analytical backgrounds or technical specialists in areas such as infrastructure, software or information architecture, who are interested in system design and engineering. Whatever your background, you’ll develop skills on some of the most advanced systems in the marketplace and enjoy opportunities to build uniquely broad experiences as you tackle challenges across our business. Our programme matches your talents to the skills required in the business in our key locations; Bournemouth, Glasgow and London.

Techonlogy Summer Internship

A Technology internship at J.P. Morgan will place you at the core of the organisation, in a culture that encourages you to think "outside the box" to achieve great results for our clients. The variety of roles offered to interns is vast, with numerous opportunities to gain technology experience across all business areas, allowing you to see if a career in technology is for you. Whatever your background, whatever your role, you will develop skills on some of the most cutting-edge systems and products in the marketplace. You'll enjoy unique experiences as you tackle and deliver on challenges across our business. Internships are offered in our Bournemouth, Glasgow and London offices. The following opportunities are available: Summer internship — 10-week long programme between June and September aimed at penultimate year undergraduate students, including students in their final year expecting to take a masters. Six-month internships — six-month industrial placements starting in April, aimed at penultimate year undergraduate students or those in their final year expecting to take a masters.

Spring Technology Week

Based at our London office, you'll spend one week learning more about Technology at J.P. Morgan. You'll meet with recent analysts who were once in your position and hear from our senior leaders. With an opportunity to work shadow with our business, you'll soon learn where your skills are best suited. Your technical skills will be put to the test during the week through a two-day Coding Challenge. Our recruiters will be on hand to answer any questions about our application process and offer practical advice on how to prepare for our assessment centres. This is an excellent way to find out early about our careers and get yourself noticed.

Open Technology Position

Our technologists work on huge IT projects, some playing a major role in securing our biggest deals. Take our Bear Stearns merger, for example. On March 16, 2008, it was announced that J.P. Morgan was acquiring Bear Stearns. The superiority of the technologies we used allowed a historic deal to take place in a matter of days. Our technology enabled us to transfer an incredible several hundred thousand transactions from Bear Stearns to J.P. Morgan over one single weekend. This is just one example from the diverse list of achievements and ongoing projects that keep our technologists challenged and engaged.

More About JP Morgan

By the time you get to the end of this sentence, we'll have changed. As the markets constantly move, so do we. Our innovation and ability to stay ahead are what give our clients the edge to meet their strategic needs, today and tomorrow. Since our inception some 200 years ago, we've evolved to offer the whole spectrum of global banking and financial services — and in many cases, helped to define them. It is the combination of character, product strength and intellectual capital that sets us apart as an industry leader. We invented the concept of relationship banking back in the nineteenth century, and we are still doing first-class business in a first-class way.

Who are we?

Today, between the Corporate & Investment Bank and Asset Management, we work together around the globe to meet the complex financial needs of corporations, governments, private firms, financial institutions, non-profit organizations and wealthy individuals. J.P. Morgan employs more than 70,000 people globally and the firm has assets of $2.3 trillion. So what does that mean for you? In short, it means huge opportunity. We offer an environment of support, security and stability where you're given the freedom to develop your career. We have offices in more than 50 countries and clients in more than 100 countries around the world — leading the industry in most markets we operate in, a testament to our global platform and leadership.
